Employment Distribution

The sectors that employ most population of a nation depend on several things like location of a country, its natural resources and its working population age. China which was predominantly an Industrial workforce and India which was an Agriculture rich nation has shown huge increase in service sector in which developed nations like Japan and USA were dominant since 1980s.
